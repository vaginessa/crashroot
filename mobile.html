About Project

Uses the QEMU exploit to root devices. This involves linking /data/tmp and /data, then setting a local.prop flag (ro.kernel.qemu=1) to push a su binary to /system/xbin.

This tool does not unlock your bootloader or install a custom recovery. It only creates the su binary, which is from SuperSU 2.0's zip.
